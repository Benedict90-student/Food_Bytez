Our Story

Discover the passion behind Food Bytez

Bringing Filipino and Chinese Flavors Together

Founded in 2023, Food Bytez was born out of a love for both Filipino and Chinese cuisine. Our founder, Chef Juan Dela Cruz, wanted to create a place where people could enjoy the best of both culinary worlds under one roof.

What started as a small food stall in Manila has now grown into a beloved restaurant known for its innovative fusion dishes and commitment to quality ingredients.

We take pride in using traditional recipes passed down through generations while adding our own modern twist to create unique flavor experiences.

Our Mission

To deliver authentic, high-quality Filipino and Chinese cuisine with fast, friendly service that brings people together through the joy of good food.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in Filipino-Chinese fusion cuisine, expanding our reach while maintaining the quality and authenticity that made us successful.
